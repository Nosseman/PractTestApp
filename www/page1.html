Welcome to PractTest App!

This app serves as my final practical test app for the Laurea UAS study unit Building and Deploying Cross Platform Mobile Apps. On this study unit we had hands-on studies how to build and deploy mobile apps targeting Android and iOS mobile devices. The apps are developed using the combination of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

On these tabs you can find some information about this app and about me. On the third tab you can check your geolocation. From the menu you find pages about my studies and of course the contact page.

Technologies used in this app include:
NodeJS, Java JDK, Apache Ant, Apache Cordova, Ionic, Android SDK.
